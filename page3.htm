Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school hours?

Classes run from 7:30 AM to 4:30 PM, Monday to Friday.

What are the school hours?

Classes run from 7:30 AM to 4:30 PM, Monday to Friday, with additional activities on Saturdays for specific programs.

What programs do you offer?

We offer strands under the Senior High School program, including STEM, ABM, HUMSS, and ICT. Learn more about each on our Strands page.

How can I contact the school?

You can reach us via our Contact Us page, email, or by calling our office during school hours.
